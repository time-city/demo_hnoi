🗺️ Improvement Roadmap

Week 1 – Foundation Review

Revisit core concepts in areas where the student scored below the class average. Focus on building a strong conceptual foundation.

Week 2 – Accuracy Training

Targeted exercises on frequently-missed question types. Emphasis on careful reading and methodical problem-solving.

Week 3 – Practice Tests

Simulate real exam conditions with timed practice tests. Identify remaining weak spots and refine strategies.

Week 4 – Reassessment

Final evaluation to measure improvement. Compare results with initial performance and adjust the learning path accordingly.
